My top 12 restaurants in San Diego, CA

Been Living in San Diego, CA a few months and so far these are some of my favorite restaurants in no particular order. hope you find one that meets your needs and enjoy them as I did.

Little Italy:

Bencotto:

Bencotto sets the bar high for fine Italian cuisine, even in the heart of Little Italy. They combine a modern-style ambiance with an Old World flavor and exhibit both those facets with their menu options, which are designed to share, but you'll want to keep yours all to yourself. "Bencotto" means cooked well: this is what authentic Italian food is about. Our menu is based on simplicity. It tastes as it should and would in Italy.Bencotto brings you the finest of italian dining right in the heart of sunny San Diego.
  • Location: 750 W Fir St #103, San Diego, CA 92101
  • Contact: (619) 450-4786

Pan Bon:

A couple of months ago a local newspaper defined Pan Bon as as a “All day Italian hub”, that’s perfectly the point. From breakfast to dinner you can enjoy an easygoing atmosphere and a hi-level service, no self-service just order and sit down, the guys will bring your meal at the table or for those who are in a hurry we pack your meal and run away. The purpose of Pan Bon is bring to San Diego the best and selected ingredients and produce daily fresh delicacies under your eyes. There is a big see-through window to the bakery.
  • Location: 1450 Kettner Blvd, San Diego, CA 92101
  • Contact: (619) 241-2443

Juniper and Ivy:

Juniper and Ivy offers “Refined America Food with Left Coast Edge” Courtesy of acclaimed Chef Richard Blais perhaps most recognizable as the Winner of Bravo’s “Top Chef All-Stars”. Blais brings his wildly creative approach to cooking and cuisine to San Diego; Backed by an All-Star Culinary Team. Excellent Cocktail and Wine Programs paired with Al Fresco Dining, High Energy Design and more makes Juniper and Ivy the newest Destination Restaurant to hit the West Coast.
  • Location: 2228 Kettner Blvd, San Diego, CA 92101
  • Contact: (619) 269-9036

Gaslamp/Downtown:

Eddie V's:

Join us at Eddie V's in San Diego and let the comfortably sophisticated environment, sparkling atmosphere, and sounds of live jazz that fill the air set the tone for an exceptional dining experience. Choose from a varied selection of the finest seafood available, or indulge in our critically acclaimed prime center cut steaks, aged 28 days and broiled to perfection. Complement your meal with a selection from our wine menu — an award-wining list of more than 200 wines curated by our Master Sommelier.
  • Location: 789 W Harbor Dr, San Diego, CA 92101
  • Contact: (619) 615-0281

Double Standard:

The Double Standard Kitchenetta and bar specializes in Modern Italian and California cuisine. One of the only restaurants in San Diego offering Wagyu-Ribeye Steak, supporting local California growers, Santa Monica Cheeses, Fresh black winter truffles, housemade pastas and Neapolitan-style pies. Double Standard Kitchenetta is a chic California restaurant .Chef Chris Gentile combines California modern flavors with cutting edge culinary techniques in unique and sophisticated dishes, while offering a trendy New York style.
  • Location: 695 Sixth Avenue, San Diego, CA 92101
  • Contact: (619) 269-9676

Nobu San Diego:

Nobu San Diego is yet another canvas for Chef Nobu Matsuhisa to display his legendary dishes, such as Black Cod with Miso and Yellowtail Jalapeno. The restaurant which is nestled in the perennially hip and centrally located Hard Rock Hotel also features unique creations by Executive Chef David Meade who has honed his skills in Nobu Miami and New York. Nobu San Diego is open for dinner nightly at the sushi bar or dining room and for drinks at the bar lounge. A private dining room is available to accommodate private parties.
  • Hard Rock Hotel San Diego, 207 Fifth Ave, San Diego, CA
  • Contact: (619) 814-4124

North Park:

Underbelly:

Underbelly North park is located at the North Parker. With an expansive, eye-catching patio on the corner at 30th Street and Upas that's dominated by set of imposing concrete tables lit with fire features that should be cozy if and when the weather ever cools down. There is seating on both sides of a bar that spans the length of the dining room. There are more bar-height seats along the open kitchen, where UnderBelly chefs prep the menu's appetizers, buns and ramen; For now, the bar stocks craft beer, wine and sake.
  • Location: 73000 Upas Street, San Diego, CA 92104
  • Contact: (619) 487-9909

The Smoking Goat:

The Smoking Goat is an award-winning California-French bistro located in the heart of North Park, San Diego. Inspired by his European Farm-to-Table up-bringing and culinary training at Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Executive Chef and Owner Fred Piehl, marries classic French cuisine with the bounty of San Diego in a way that is rustic, yet refined, imaginative, yet unpretentious. The dinners served feature in-season local, sustainable and organic ingredients and provide guests with a unique culinary experience.
  • Location: 3408 30th Street, San Diego, CA 92104
  • Contact: (619) 955-5295

One Door North:

30th Street's newest food addition is a second offering from tried-and-true neighborhood restaurateurs. One Door North will offer craft cocktails and at least 40 wines, and will have a special retail area for off-site bottle sales. In the completely revamped kitchen, There are pizzas, burgers and salads, but the seasonal dishes also include cast iron-roasted vegetables and playful plates that evoke cooking in the great outdoors, from campfire trout with cornbread and coleslaw to a s'mores dessert.
  • Location: 3422 30th Street, San Diego, CA 92104
  • Contact: (619) 618-1285

Pacific Beach:

The Patio Lamont Str:

Voted Best Neighborhood Restaurant, The Patio on Lamont offers worldly wines, cool cocktails, craft beers and a chef-inspired, sustainably sourced menu. Relax by the fire on our Patio, in the Bar with friends or our comfortable Loft with a date. Enjoy local craft Beer, specialty Cocktails and our funky Wine list. Share dishes from our eclectic small plate menu. Join us for brunch (Saturday and Sunday), lunch, happy hour, and of course dinner. We offer military and first responder discounts. Ocean and Pet Friendly.
  • Location: 4445 Lamont Street, San Diego, CA 92109
  • Contact: (858) 412-4648

JRDN Restaurant:

JRDN features hand selected, all-natural meats, the finest seafood from sustainable sources, organic and locally grown produce. Each creation is thoughtfully prepared for your culinary delight. JRDN Bar and Lounge is a social indoor and outdoor venue offering a unique experience that is both casual and sophisticated. The atmosphere is warm and inviting and the crowd, hip yet comfortable - all enjoying the view of the ocean while sipping hand-crafted cocktails and exploring one of San Diego's most creative wine lists.
  • Location: TOWER23 Hotel, 723 Felspar St, San Diego, CA 92109
  • Contact: (858) 270-5736

Backyard Kitchen & Tap:

Backyard Kitchen and Tap is a chic, yet cozily coastal American eatery . With homemade specialties and an extensive list of craft cocktails and local beers to choose from, Backyard Kitchen and Tap offers the perfect culinary experience for everyone. For a tasty brunch (Backyard Benedict, anyone?), lunch or dinner, Backyard satisfies every palate with scrumptious American favorites and spirits. From our signature selection of flatbreads to our array of steak and fresh fish entrees, you’ll discover a long list of reasons to return for more!
  • Location: 832 Garnet Avenue, San Diego, CA 92109
  • Contact: (858) 859-2593
